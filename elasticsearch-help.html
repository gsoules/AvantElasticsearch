Search Tips
  • You can use the following special characters in the Keywords field and for the Contains condition:
  • + before a keyword makes it required
  • - before a keyword excludes it
  • " wraps a phrase to search for
  • | signifies OR
  • * after a keyword signifies a wildcard
  • ( and ) signify precedence
  • For an open-ended year range such as from 1900 or until 1970, leave either Start or End blank
  • To search by Contributor, use the organization's Id from the Contributors page
